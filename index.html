    About Me

    Dr. Kasi Gorli is a healthcare analytics professional with a clinical foundation in dentistry and a Master's in Health Informatics. With 3 years of experience in healthcare data analytics, he brings a unique blend of clinical insight, technical skill, and a passion for improving patient outcomes through data. As part of his recent internship with Vandalia Health, Dr. Gorli designed clinical dashboards using real-world EHR data in Tableau, including a hospitalist performance dashboard and a Population Health Compliance Dashboard aligned with HEDIS metrics. He is also the co-author of research exploring ransomware impacts on hospital operations and expanding telestroke access in underserved communities. Actively pursuing certifications in Python, predictive modelling, and Tableau, Dr. Gorli is focused on deepening his skills in real-time analytics, risk stratification, and health equity analysis.
